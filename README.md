## 🔧 How it works

deadswitch uses a dead-man-switch style flow.

1. You set your check-in schedule.
2. You add the wallet or wallets you want to protect.
3. You choose the person or people who should get access later.
4. You keep checking in through the app.
5. If you stop, the app starts the handoff process based on your rules.

This keeps the plan simple. You do not need to manage it every day. You only need to check in on the schedule you set.

If the check-in period ends, your family member may need to follow the claim steps you set up.

Make sure they know:

- Where you saved the instructions
- Which wallet or wallets are covered
- What name or contact they should use
- How to reach the person who helps with the claim
- Where the backup details are stored

Keep the instructions simple. Short steps are easier to follow during a stressful time.

## 🛠️ Common Windows issues

### The app will not open

- Right-click the file and choose Run as administrator
- Check that Windows did not block the file
- Make sure you downloaded the full release

### The app looks blank

- Close the app and open it again
- Check your internet connection
- Make sure your display scale is set to a standard value

### The wallet will not connect

- Confirm your wallet app is open
- Check that you are on the right network
- Refresh the app and try again

### The check-in screen does not update

- Make sure the app still has internet access
- Confirm the date and time on your PC are correct
- Open the app again and sign back in
